Learning intentions

This module will demonstrate how explicit teaching can be used as a way to develop inclusive practices, student agency, mathematical dialogue, regular feedback, and cognitive activation strategies.

In this module you will:

  • develop an understanding of how explicit teaching can be used to enhance their practice and knowledge of students
  • explore the seven key components which are considered essential in teaching and learning.

Success criteria

After completing Module 1 teachers will demonstrate their understanding by reflecting on the key components and by brainstorming ways to embed the seven components into their planning documentation and practice.
